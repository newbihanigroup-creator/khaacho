# Credit Scoring System

## Overview

The Khaacho platform includes an automated credit scoring engine that evaluates retailer creditworthiness based on their transaction history and behavior patterns. Scores range from 300 (very poor) to 900 (excellent) and are calculated using a weighted multi-factor model.

## Score Range

- **300-449**: Very Poor - High risk, recommend cash-only transactions
- **450-549**: Poor - Below average, consider reducing credit limits
- **550-649**: Fair - Acceptable with monitoring
- **650-749**: Good - Reliable credit standing
- **750-900**: Excellent - Eligible for credit limit increases
